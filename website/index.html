AWESOME FEATURES

Here's a cross-section of our awesome app features and user friendly interface!

Sign-up/Sign-in

A User friendly Page to Sign up or Log in if you already have a Learnalign account.
You can also log in using Google or Facebook

Featuring an eye-catching yet ambient design, our sign-up/sign-in page has an easily navigated user interface with user convinient prompts such as registering directly with your Mail, Google account or Facebook page.
It also features a link to retrieve forgotten passwords or inaccessible accounts.

Courses

This page gives users access to their current courses, Class groups, Facilitators and coursemates for interactive purposes

Users can also recieve better tips for Learning and Collaborations

Commendations and status reports View Overall Progress and add New courses to their curriculum

Home

Our Homepage features user friendly prompts to get started with daily tasks, sections for users to Find new tasks and Interests, Join Classes conviniently and select study categories and previously registered courses.

Users can also find supplementary course recommendations to fine tune thier 21st century learning, life and literacy skills

Profile

Our profile page allows for users to view daily tasks, track progress, recieve updates and Find new courses and communities to Interact with. Users can find intern listings online and when advanced to the required level, find paid jobs online using just their app qualifications.

Users can also participate in competitions, refer a friend, earn discounts on tuition, claim rewards and unlock new achievements.
